Claims & Substantiation Checklist

‚úÖ Verify All Metrics Before Publishing

  • Reply rates: Must be based on pilot data with a clear sample size (e.g., "12% reply rate from a pilot of 50 agencies").
  • Time savings: Must be backed by internal studies or credible industry benchmarks.
  • Intros delivered: Must be confirmed with actual data from the platform.
  • Market size/growth: Must reference specific, credible U.S. sources from the last 12 months.
  • AI/LLM claims: Must be truthful and avoid exaggeration. Clearly state what is AI-powered and what is not.
