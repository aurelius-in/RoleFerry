RoleFerry Fast FAQ for Agencies

Answer the top 12 questions agencies ask

What does RoleFerry do vs. doesn't do today?
RoleFerry turns job descriptions into personalized outreach that gets responses. We don't do CRM/ATS deep integrations, advanced analytics dashboards, or multi-language support yet - those are roadmap items.
What's the difference between job seeker vs. recruiter modes?
Same core technology, different interface. Job seekers see "Job Preferences" and "Candidate Profile" tabs. Recruiters see "Ideal Client Profile" and "Resume Database" tabs. Both get the same 5-step workflow.
Why limit to 3 contacts per company by default?
Prevents spam, maintains deliverability, and looks professional. You can contact multiple people at the same company, but not too many to avoid looking unprofessional.
How is the fit score calculated?
AI analyzes your skills against job requirements, matches pain points to your solutions, and gives a percentage score with plain-English rationale explaining why you match (or don't).
What are pre-flight checks and what does "green" mean?
Built-in deliverability checks ensure your emails reach the inbox. Green ticks mean your email will likely be delivered successfully. Red means there are issues to fix before sending.
How does RoleFerry handle data and privacy?
We use mock data in demos, no PII in examples. Real users' data is encrypted, GDPR compliant, and never shared. We're SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified.
What's the difference between the tracker vs. CSV export?
The in-app tracker shows your application pipeline with stages and outcomes. CSV export lets you download data for external analysis or reporting.
What's on the roadmap for CRM/ATS integrations?
Deep integrations with Bullhorn, CEIPAL, and other major platforms are planned for Q2 2024. We'll start with API connections and build from there.
How does the AI actually work?
We use GPT-4 to extract pain points from job descriptions, match them to your skills, and generate personalized outreach. It's like having a personal recruiter for every application.
What makes RoleFerry different from other job platforms?
We're the only platform that turns job descriptions into personalized outreach. Others just match keywords - we match pain points to solutions and craft targeted messages.

What's the onboarding process like?
Simple 5-step setup: paste a job description, we extract the details, you confirm the match, select contacts, and launch. Most users are sending their first campaign within 15 minutes.
