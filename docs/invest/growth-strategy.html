Growth Strategy

Go-to-Market & Scaling Plans

Phase 1: Foundation (Q1-Q2 2026)

  • Pilot Program: Validate core 10-step workflow with 10-15 initial pilot customers in Charleston, SC and Austin, TX.
  • Customer Acquisition: Target 50 new mid-market companies and staffing agencies.
  • Key Metrics: Achieve 10-18% reply rates, 1-3 qualified intros per pilot in 14 days.
  • Hiring Trigger: Solidify core founding team (2-3 FTEs).

Phase 2: Scale (Q3 2026)

  • U.S. Market Penetration: Expand sales and marketing efforts into the broader Texas and South Carolina markets.
  • Product Enhancement: Launch advanced analytics and begin lightweight CRM/ATS integrations (export/webhooks).
  • Key Metrics: Achieve $500K ARR, 500+ qualified intros delivered.
  • Hiring Trigger: Expand sales (1-2 FTEs) and engineering (1-2 FTEs) teams.

Phase 3: Expansion (Q4 2026)

  • National Rollout: Begin targeting key accounts across the U.S. market.
  • Enterprise Features: Launch enterprise-grade features like SSO and advanced security.
  • Key Metrics: Achieve $1M ARR, 1000+ qualified intros delivered, maintain 15%+ reply rates.
  • Hiring Trigger: Scale to 10-15 employees across all functions.
