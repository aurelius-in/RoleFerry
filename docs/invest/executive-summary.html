Our Mission

To eliminate the friction in recruitment by using AI to create perfect matches between talent and opportunity, making the hiring process faster, more accurate, and more human.

Our Solution

10-Step AI-Powered Workflow
(One-click automation when data is present)

RoleFerry's proprietary workflow guides users from identifying a hiring signal to launching a personalized outreach campaign, automating the most time-consuming parts of business development for staffing agencies.

  • Market Signal Intel: Identify companies with hiring needs.
  • Pain Point Matching: AI-driven alignment between your value and their need.
  • Contact Discovery: Find the right decision-maker.
  • Campaign Compose & Launch: Build and send hyper-personalized outreach.
  • Pipeline Tracking: Manage new opportunities in a simple Kanban view.
