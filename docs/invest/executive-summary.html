Our Solution

10-Tab Intelligent Workflow

RoleFerry's proprietary 10-tab workflow system guides users through a comprehensive process:

  • Job Preferences/ICP: Define ideal roles and target companies
  • Resume/Candidate Profile: AI-powered profile analysis and optimization
  • Job Descriptions: Intelligent job posting analysis and pain point extraction
  • Pain Point Matching: AI-driven alignment scoring between candidates and roles
  • Contact Discovery: Automated contact finding with email verification
  • Context Research: Company and contact research with AI summarization
  • Offer Creation: Personalized pitch asset generation
  • Email Composition: AI-powered email writing with tone adaptation
  • Campaign Management: Multi-step email sequence automation
  • Deliverability & Launch: Pre-flight checks and campaign execution

Key Differentiators

AI-First Approach

Unlike traditional ATS systems, RoleFerry uses advanced AI for every step of the process, from profile analysis to email generation.

Dual-Mode Architecture

Single platform serving both job seekers and recruiters with dynamic UI adaptation and context-sensitive workflows.

End-to-End Automation

Complete workflow automation from job discovery to outreach execution, reducing manual effort by 80%.
